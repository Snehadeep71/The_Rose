What is the Artistry behind Rose’s Culinary Craft?

Nestled in the city of joy, Kolkata, The Rose stands as a beam of culinary excellence, where each dish is not merely a meal but a masterpiece crafted with artistry and passion. Our commitment to serve excellence, initiates with the founder’s passion and upheld for 27 years with our talented chefs, whose creativity and dedication unfolds magic in our kitchen. Our founder’s background in design and European pastry arts infuses each creation with innovation and a dedication to serving unforgettable dining experiences. Our artistry lies in crafting a menu that tells a cohesive story, whether it's inspired by regional cuisines, seasonal themes, or unique culinary concepts, requires thoughtful planning and creativity and in sourcing and selecting the best quality ingredients, often locally and seasonally, to ensure freshness, flavor, and sustainability.

The Rose: Where Culinary Passion Blossoms into Unforgettable Experiences

The vibrant eatery invites you to a gastronomic journey across continents, from the robust spices of North India to the delicate flavors of China, the rich traditions of South India, and the comforting classics of Italy. At ‘The Rose’, culinary artistry transcends the mere act of cooking; it is a passion, a dedication, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Whether you are celebrating a special occasion or simply savoring a meal with loved ones, our restaurant invites you to experience the magic of our founder’s vision brought to life on every plate. Join us and discover why The Rose is more than just a restaurant—it is a culinary destination where every meal tells a story of craftsmanship, innovation, and the art of creating unforgettable memories.

Seeking Culinary Bliss? Experience The Rose's Flavorful Odyssey!

Step in to get a passport to culinary delight! Be greeted by the tantalizing aroma of North Indian spices that evoke memories of bustling markets and vibrant streets. Indulge in succulent Tandoori kebabs, rich and creamy curries like our signature Butter Chicken, and the smoky goodness of Seekh Kebabs. Each dish is a testament to the bold and aromatic flavors that define North Indian cuisine Or for those with a penchant for the exotic flavors of the East, our Chinese menu promises a culinary adventure. Experience the umami explosion of General Tso's Chicken, the delicate flavors of Dim Sum, or the comforting warmth of hot and sour soup. our menu, inspired by the region's diverse culinary tradition offers the spicy delights of Chettinad cuisine to the comforting embrace of a Masala Dosa, each dish captures the authentic flavors of Kerala, Tamil Nadu, and Andhra Pradesh. Discover the rich tapestry of South Indian cuisine right here in kankurgachi, Kolkata. You can choose to transport yourself to Italy with our selection of pasta and pizza offerings. Whether you crave the creamy indulgence of Fettuccine Alfredo or the savory perfection of a custom pizza, our Italian menu promises to satisfy every craving for classic Mediterranean flavors. "Sweet tooth calling? We've got just the treats to satisfy your cravings!" Treat yourself to our irresistible confectionery section by Indulging in decadent cakes like our famous Red Velvet, melt-in-the-mouth chocolate truffles, or the comforting nostalgia of a classic apple pie. Our dessert offerings are a paradise for anyone with a sweet tooth, ensuring a delightful end to your dining experience.

What Measures Ensure Health and Hygiene Excellence at Our Restaurant?

At The Rose, we uphold stringent measures to guarantee impeccable standards of health and hygiene throughout our establishment. Our commitment to cleanliness and safety is paramount, ensuring every guest enjoys a dining experience free from worry. Elevating every dish with fresh and quality ingredients We firmly believe that exceptional dishes begin with exceptional ingredients. From the vibrant produce sourced locally to the finest imported spices and meats, our commitment to quality is evident in every bite. Each ingredient is carefully selected to ensure freshness, flavor, and a culinary experience that surpasses expectations. Our kitchen is a testament to our dedication, where skilled chefs transform these ingredients into culinary masterpieces that delight the senses. Cleaning Protocols Our restaurant undergoes rigorous cleaning routines daily, with surfaces, utensils, and equipment sanitation, adhering to the Norms of Food Safety and Hygiene of India. Additional compliance with local health regulations and guidelines, ensures that our kitchen and dining areas proffer optimal cleanliness and safety standards. Embracing excellence with best practices Our team members undergo regular training sessions to stay informed about the latest industry standards and practices. This ensures they are equipped with the knowledge and skills needed to uphold our commitment to excellence.Beyond operational efficiency, we are committed to sustainable practices that minimize our environmental footprint. This includes initiatives such as reducing food waste, sourcing locally whenever possible, and promoting eco-friendly packaging options.
